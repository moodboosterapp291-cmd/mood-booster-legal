# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 30, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Mood Booster ("we", "our", "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our minimal data practices.

**Contact:** moodboosterapp291@gmail.com
**Location:** Germany

## 2. Our Privacy-First Approach

Mood Booster is designed with privacy as a core principle. **All your personal content — including mood entries, journal entries ("3 Good Things"), course progress, and statistics — is stored locally on your device.** We do not collect, transmit, or store this content on our servers.

## 3. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be explicit about what we **do not** collect:

- We do not require account registration
- We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
- We do not collect or transmit your mood entries, journal content, or any text you write in the App
- We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or precise location
- We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites
- We do not sell or share data with advertisers
- We do not use behavioral analytics or marketing trackers

## 4. Information We Do Collect

To keep the App working reliably, we collect a minimal amount of technical information:

### 4.1 Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics)
When the App crashes, we collect anonymous technical information to help us fix bugs:
- Device model and operating system version
- App version
- Stack trace of the crash (technical information about what failed in the code)
- Approximate time of the crash

This data is processed by Google Firebase Crashlytics on behalf of us. It does not contain your personal content (mood entries, journal entries, etc.) and cannot be used to identify you personally.

**Legal basis (GDPR):** Legitimate interest in maintaining a stable, functional application (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

### 4.2 Push Notification Token (if notifications are enabled)
If you enable push notifications, your device generates an anonymous token that allows us to send notifications to your device. This token is not linked to your identity.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

## 5. Where Your Data Is Stored

- **Personal content (mood, journal, progress):** Stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to it.
- **Crash reports:** Processed and stored by Google Firebase. Firebase servers are located globally, including in the United States. By using the App, you understand that crash data may be transferred to the United States. Google participates in the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework, which provides safeguards for such transfers.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Personal content:** Retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.
- **Crash reports:** Retained by Firebase for up to 90 days, after which they are automatically deleted.

## 7. Your Rights Under GDPR (for users in the European Economic Area)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights:

- **Right of access:** Since all personal content is stored on your device, you have direct access to it through the App.
- **Right to erasure:** You can delete any entry directly in the App, or uninstall the App to remove all local data.
- **Right to object:** You can opt out of crash reporting in the App settings (if available) or by uninstalling the App.
- **Right to lodge a complaint:** You may contact your local data protection authority if you believe we have violated your rights.

To exercise any of these rights or ask questions, contact us at moodboosterapp291@gmail.com.

## 8. Your Rights Under CCPA (for California residents)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- **Right to know:** What personal information we collect (described in Section 4)
- **Right to delete:** Delete personal data (uninstall the App or contact us)
- **Right to non-discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
- **Right to opt out of sale:** We do not sell your personal information

To exercise these rights, contact us at moodboosterapp291@gmail.com.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect any information from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has used the App, please contact us at moodboosterapp291@gmail.com and we will delete any associated data.

## 10. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Google Firebase (Crashlytics):** Crash reporting. [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

We do not integrate with any other third-party services that collect personal data.

## 11. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data:
- Personal content is stored on your device using the operating system's standard storage protections
- Crash reports are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
